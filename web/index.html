Attendance tracking with QR scanning and Google Sheets sync.

QR Asistencia is an internal attendance management application used to register worker attendance through QR code scans. The application helps authorized staff record check-ins, review operational history, and optionally synchronize attendance logs to a Google Spreadsheet selected by the user.

Who uses it Authorized internal users responsible for attendance administration and operational follow-up.
What it stores Attendance records such as worker ID, name, project, date, time, logged-in user, and event type.
Why Google access is requested To create or connect a Google Spreadsheet and append attendance records generated by the app.

Verification Summary

Public information without login

This page is accessible without requiring users to sign in to the application.

Google user data purpose
  • Create a spreadsheet during setup when needed.
  • Connect a spreadsheet explicitly selected by the user.
  • Append attendance rows generated from QR scans.
Data minimization

The application does not use Google user data for advertising, profiling, selling data, or unrelated services such as Gmail or Contacts.

App Functions

The application is designed for internal attendance workflows. It records attendance events, keeps an auditable history, and supports administrative review of operational records.

QR attendance flow

Workers are identified through QR-based scanning to generate attendance events.

History review

Authorized users can inspect attendance history for operational control and follow-up.

Sheets synchronization

Attendance rows can be written to a Google Spreadsheet chosen by the authorized user.

How Google Data Is Used

Google access is requested only when an authorized user wants to use Google Sheets as the export destination for attendance records.

Requested access

Google Sign-In and Google Sheets access for spreadsheet setup, connection, and row creation.

Operational use

Write attendance information such as worker ID, name, project, date, time, logged-in user, and event.

Scope of use

The data is used only for attendance administration and internal reporting related to the app.
