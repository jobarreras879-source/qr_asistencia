App Functions

What the application does

QR Asistencia allows authorized users to scan worker QR codes, register attendance events, keep an operational record of those events, and optionally export attendance information to Google Sheets for internal administrative control.

QR attendance registration

Workers are identified through QR code scans to generate attendance records.

History and review

Authorized staff can review attendance history for operational tracking and internal reporting.

How Google user data is used

  • Google Sheets setup The app may create or connect a spreadsheet chosen by the authorized user.
  • Attendance export The app writes attendance rows such as worker ID, name, project, date, time, logged-in user, and attendance event.
  • Limited purpose The data is used only for attendance administration and internal operational reporting.

Important limitations

The application does not use Google user data for advertising, profiling, selling data, or unrelated Google services such as Gmail or Contacts.
